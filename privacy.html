Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Pinlist is a student research project designed to generate music recommendations based on user-authorized Pinterest data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled.

Information Collected

When a user connects their Pinterest account through the official Pinterest API, the application may access:

  • Board titles and descriptions
  • Pin titles and descriptions (if available)
  • Public metadata associated with saved pins

Only data explicitly authorized by the user through OAuth is accessed.

How Information Is Used

The data is used solely to analyze thematic preferences and generate personalized Spotify music recommendations.

No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Pinterest data is processed for recommendation generation. The application does not permanently store personal Pinterest content unless explicitly stated in future updates.

Third-Party Services

This project integrates with:

  • Pinterest API
  • Spotify Web API

Use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

User Rights

Users may revoke application access at any time through their Pinterest or Spotify account settings.
