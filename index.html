What is PinClip?

A research project that reads the visual language of your Pinterest boards — colors, subjects, moods — and maps that aesthetic fingerprint to a musical profile, generating a playlist that feels like your board sounds.

01

Visual Analysis

CLIP encodes every pin image into a mood probability across 9 aesthetic categories.

02

Profile Mapping

Mood probabilities are weighted and translated into a set of quantified musical characteristics.

03

Recommendation

Candidate tracks are surfaced and filtered by a language model for tonal accuracy.

Musical Dimensions

Each board generates a unique blend across these characteristics:

  • Mood · emotional tone
  • Energy · intensity & drive
  • Rhythm · danceability
  • Texture · acoustic vs. electronic
  • Tempo · beats per minute
  • Tonality · major vs. minor
  • Instrumentation · vocal presence
  • Dynamics · loudness range
